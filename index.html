About

I am an AI Scientist at Pigment, where I develop AI agents that automate financial planning, forecasting, and business decision-making.

Previously, I was a machine learning scientist at NuMind, contributing to NuExtract, a family of LLMs for multi-modal information extraction. My research focuses on language models, evaluation, and information retrieval.

I hold a PhD from CNRS (LORIA), where I researched controllable and document-level text simplification. Before my PhD, I worked as a machine learning engineer at Notiv (acquired by Dubber) and completed my bachelors and honours degrees at Queensland University of Technology.
