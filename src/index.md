## About

I am an AI Scientist at [Pigment](https://www.pigment.com), where I work on AI agents for automating financial planning, forecasting, and business decision-making workflows.

Previously, I was a machine learning scientist at [NuMind](https://numind.ai), contributing to [NuExtract](https://huggingface.co/numind), a family of LLMs for multi-modal information extraction. My research interests include language models, evaluation, and information retrieval.

I hold a PhD from [CNRS (LORIA)](https://www.loria.fr), where I researched controllable and document-level text simplification. Before my PhD, I worked as a machine learning engineer at [Notiv](http://notiv.com) (acquired by [Dubber](https://www.dubber.net)) and completed my bachelors and honours degrees at [Queensland University of Technology](https://www.qut.edu.au).
